## 2. Credits & festivals

**Ionuț Octavian Ghinea**
- Film: "După dealuri" (Beyond the Hills, 2012, dir. Cristian Mungiu) — role "Ionuț" (the girl's brother), cast at 23; film double-awarded at Cannes. IMDb: https://www.imdb.com/name/nm4944398/ ; CineMagia (7.9/10): https://www.cinemagia.ro/actori/ionut-ghinea-17266/
- Education: Liceul de Arte "Margareta Sterian" Buzău (2008); UNATC "I.L. Caragiale" — Actorie (2011, entered 3rd on admission list); Master Arta actorului, UAT Târgu Mureș (2012–2014); Master Teatru de animație/regie, UNATC (2019–2021).
- Theater employment: Teatrul Țăndărică (collaborator, 2011–2012); Teatrul Metropolis București (2016–present); actor + director at Teatrul de Păpuși "Cărăbuș" Brăila (~1.5 years, from 2021); one year teaching at Liceul de Arte "Margareta Sterian".
- Director credits: "Frumoasa și Bestia" (Cărăbuș Brăila, 2021 — still in repertoire); at Teatrul George Ciprian Buzău: "Lampa lui Aladin" (premiere 30 Oct 2022 — sold out in under 4 days: https://www.teatrulgeorgeciprian.ro/lampa-lui-aladin-cea-mai-recenta-premira-a-teatrului-george-ciprian-in-regia-lui-ionut-octavian-ghinea/), "Cartea Junglei" (director + Mowgli), "Capra cu trei iezi" (still running Mar 2026), plus "Cenușăreasa" listings on buzaumedia.ro.

**Ana-Maria Pușcălău**
- UNATC "I.L. Caragiale" graduate, Păpuși-Marionete programme, class of 2011.
- Assistant director on all three George Ciprian productions above; co-founder, co-producer and performer at Teatrul Copilăriei; co-star of "Rapunzel" (1 Iunie show at Teatrul George Ciprian, free entry funded by Buzău County Council).

**Festivals**
- Puppets Occupy Street Festival, Craiova (Aug 2020) — "Rapunzel" and "Lampa lui Aladdin", 4 performances each: https://puppetsoccupystreet.blogspot.com/2020/08/poftiti-la-teatrul-copilariei.html . Blog notes theater founded ~2014, repertoire 25+ shows.
